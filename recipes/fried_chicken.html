Fried Chicken Recipe

Description

Fried chicken is a classic dish that is very easy to make. Almost everyone has their own
unique recipe whether it be breaded or battered fried chicken. This recipe is for classic battered fried chicken that
leaves you with a clean, reuseable fryng oil after each fry ready for the next batch.

The secret to a well-seasoned fried chicken is brining. It ensures that the seasoning
penetrates well within the meat and not just on the skin. Brine your chicken in a salt solution for 30 minutes up to 1 hr
before frying.

Ingredients

Brine

  • 1 kg chicken pieces
  • 2 tbsp salt
  • 500 ml water

Batter

  • 0.5 cup flour
  • 0.5 cup cornstarch
  • 420 ml water
  • 1 medium egg
  • 1.5 tbsp pepper
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 0.5 tsp turmeric for color
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • 1 L oil for frying

Directions

  1. For the brine, mix salt and water. Soak chicken for 30 minutes up to 1 hour.
  2. Prepare the batter. In a bowl, mix all the batter ingredients except the oil.
  3. Put brined chicken in the batter. Heat oil in a deep frying pot to 160C.
  4. Drain excess batter back to the bowl before frying. Fry chicken in batches.
    then reduce temperature to 130C.
  5. Fry for 10 - 15 minutes.
  6. Drain excess oil in paper towel then serve while warm and crispy.
